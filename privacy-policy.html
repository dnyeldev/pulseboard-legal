Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

PulseBoard ("we," "our," or "the app") is a real-time intelligence dashboard for the Philippines, developed by Daniel ("the developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using PulseBoard, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Passwords are securely hashed and stored by our authentication provider (Supabase). We may also collect your display name if you choose to provide one.

Location Data

With your permission, PulseBoard accesses your device's location to provide localized weather forecasts, nearby alerts, and relevant regional data. Location data is used in real time and is not stored on our servers. You can revoke location access at any time through your device settings.

AI Chat Messages

When you use the AI Intel feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. We may store chat history to provide conversation continuity. Messages are associated with your account but are not shared with third parties beyond the AI processing provider.

Usage and Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage data and crash reports to improve app performance and stability. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, and interaction patterns. This data cannot be used to personally identify you.

Subscription Data

If you subscribe to PulseBoard Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) through RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment card details. We receive only subscription status, purchase date, and expiration information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the PulseBoard experience (weather, alerts, news, and other widgets)
  • To process and respond to your AI Intel queries
  • To manage your account and subscription status
  • To improve app performance through anonymized analytics
  • To send important service-related notifications (with your consent)

3. Third-Party Services

PulseBoard uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-powered insights (privacy policy)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management (privacy policy)
  • OpenWeatherMap — weather data (privacy policy)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — payment processing

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. AI chat history is retained to provide conversation continuity and may be periodically cleared. Analytics data is retained in anonymized form. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

6. Children's Privacy

PulseBoard is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen (More → Settings → Delete Account). This will permanently remove your account data, including your profile, preferences, and AI chat history. Anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of PulseBoard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
