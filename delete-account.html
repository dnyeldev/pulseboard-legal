Delete Your Account

PulseBoard — Philippine Intelligence Dashboard

In the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in PulseBoard:

  • Open PulseBoard
  • Go to the More tab
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

By Email

If you can't access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing:

support@allelicmedia.com

Include the email address associated with your PulseBoard account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials and profile
  • Chat history with Pulse AI
  • Widget preferences and settings
  • Notification tokens
  • All data stored in our systems

Active subscriptions should be cancelled separately through Google Play or the App Store to avoid continued billing.
